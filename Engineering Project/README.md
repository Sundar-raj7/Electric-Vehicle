The Automated Solar Powered Grass Cutter is an eco-friendly and energy-efficient solution designed to automate lawn maintenance. |
This system utilizes solar energy to power a robotic grass-cutting mechanism, reducing dependency on conventional electricity and manual labor.
The project integrates renewable energy with automation to create a sustainable and intelligent grass-cutting system suitable for gardens, lawns, and agricultural fields.

**Components Used:**

Solar Panel

Rechargeable Battery

DC Motors

Motor Driver Circuit

Microcontroller (ArduinoUNO)

Cutting Blade Mechanism

Chassis (robot frame)

Wheels and Gear System

Sensors

**Working Principle**

The solar panel converts sunlight into electrical energy.

This energy is stored in a battery.

The microcontroller controls the motors for movement and cutting.

The cutting blade rotates using a DC motor to trim grass.

The system can be enhanced with sensors to detect obstacles and navigate automatically.
